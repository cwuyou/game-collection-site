Cookie Clicker Pro

By OnlineGames.io

Love cookies? Want to create the biggest cookie empire the internet has ever seen? Then Cookie Clicker Pro is exactly your game! It's a simple, addictively fun incremental game, packed with sweet strategies. Let’s dive right in!

Cookie Clicker Pro is an idle clicker game where players click on the giant cookie to bake, invest in upgrades and buildings, and automate cookie production. The aim is to grow from a small bakery into a massive cookie tycoon with lots of buildings, achievements, upgrades, and many more.

Control

Use your mouse to play Cookie Clicker Pro.

How To Play Cookie Clicker Pro

Click on the giant cookie. Every click adds fresh-baked cookies to your stash. You can track the number of your cookies at the top of your screen. You can also read the news ticker with jokes about the impact you created by baking.  

Once you've clicked enough, buy your first buildings to automate cookie-making. Start with buying cursors, and they will keep clicking for you. Next, employ some Grandmas, they bake slowly but steadily. When you progress enough with cursors and grandmas, you'll open factories, farms, and even cookie mines to pump out mountains of cookies every second.

Keep building and watch your cookie empire expand like crazy! Use your cookies to buy upgrades, doubling your baking pow(d)er. Upgrades turn your humble bakery into an unstoppable cookie-producing machine.

Game Windows

Mastering the game means knowing your windows. Here’s the quick scoop, you can access them at the bottom of your screen:

  • Buildings: Purchase automated structures. Buy individually or bulk-buy with easy tabs.
  • Upgrades: Boost your efficiency; every upgrade makes a specific type of building more productive.
  • Stats: Track your achievements, cookies per second, and Sugar Lumps earned.
  • Settings: Adjust sounds and visuals, and enter your secret bonus codes here.
  • Mini Game: Access the games from the building window. Unlock and play mini-games for extra Sugar Lumps and fun twists that help your empire flourish even faster. Wack the mole, Cookieman trivia, and many more await you.
  • Help: Need guidance? Read the quick tutorials to refresh your knowledge.

Who's CookieMan in Cookie Clicker Pro?

Long ago, there was a magical place called the Great Cookie Realm. Here, cookies grew on trees, milk flowed in rivers, and life was peaceful. CookieMan, a giant protector made from cookie dough, watched over this world. His job was to keep cookie-baking balanced so the magic wouldn’t disappear. 

One day, players from Cookie Clicker Pro found a portal to this peaceful land. They baked cookies too fast, building factories and machines, which threatened the realm’s magic. CookieMan warned them to slow down, but they didn’t listen. So, CookieMan entered the world of Cookie Clicker Pro to stop the cookie-making madness. You will fight against CookieMan by baking more and more. But be warned, he'll try to stop you!

The Original Cookie Clicking Game

In 2013, the very first Cookie Clicker by Orteil, introduced players to a simple but strangely addictive concept of incremental games. In a very simple webpage, you click on a giant cookie, earn a few cookies, and spend them on new ways to produce more cookies. That original HTML game became a phenomenon, setting the stage for the entire “idle clicker” genre. Players loved how easy it was to learn: just keep clicking, and watch your cookie count skyrocket.

What's new in Cookie Clicker Pro?

Cookie Clicker Pro takes that original idea and cranks it up. Romanian indie game company FreezeNova developed Cookie Clicker Pro to make the cookie-clicking experience deeper and more interactive. This newer version, built in Unity, was released 11 years after the original.

It keeps the original’s idea but adds more features, like an improved ascension system and mini-games like trivia, whackamole, and cookie runner, besides better graphics and a richer gameplay experience. Not only do you collect cookies and upgrades, but you also get a rich story involving CookieMan, who protects a magical cookie realm from unbalanced baking.

In Cookie Clicker Pro, your progress feels more rewarding, thanks to additional upgrades and the Prestige system. Unlockable cheats give you quick boosts when you need them, and ascension mechanics let you start over with permanent bonuses, pushing you toward even bigger cookie counts. It’s like stepping into a sequel that takes everything you loved about the first Cookie Clicker and adds new layers of strategy, story, and creativity, and fun. Overall, it’s a fresh spin on the idle clicker genre for anyone who wants more variety and depth than the original.
