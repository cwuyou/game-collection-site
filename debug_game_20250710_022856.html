Find the One for You!

Horoscopes, tarot cards, crystal balls, or dream readings—humans have been curious about the unknown since the dawn of time. We have developed endless methods just to glimpse the future and act accordingly. From interpreting patterns in tea leaves, coffee grounds, or wine sediments, cultures have found various ways to predict future events.

Loves Me, Loves Me Not!

However, as we immerse ourselves in the digital world of the 21st century, we replace old-school fortune-telling methods with new ones. Inevitably, love testers also evolve beyond the traditional games like Loves Me, Loves Me Not! Love Tester Story offers a fun and interactive experience that lets you test your crush's feelings. No more picking leaves from daisies, no more complex tarot readings, or endless astrology interpretations. Just enter your name in Love Tester Story and get love compatibility results in percentage.

But that's not all! Love Tester Story features a unique story mode where you can reveal the story of high school girl Grace by making decisions on her behalf. If you're intrigued, keep reading to learn more about Love Tester Story.

What to Expect from Love Tester Story?

Story Mode

The story mode is the most romantic of all. You'll step into the shoes of high school girl Grace and make decisions about her love life. You'll learn valuable life lessons about love, friendship, and courage. Your choices matter! Enter the mode, choose your path, and reveal one of the multiple endings of this love story. Mix and match your decisions to explore all the endings.

The Perfect Match

In this mode, you'll be given a random name and try to find a perfect match for that name based on your instincts. Who would date whom? If you trust your matchmaking skills, try this mode, and you'll have a hard time stopping giggling.

Love Tester Mode

The third game mode is the original love tester with improved accuracy. Enter your name and your crush's name, press the big heart on the screen, and retrieve the results in seconds. If you'd like to stir things up a bit, enter the names of the couples you know and reveal the secrets of their relationships.
