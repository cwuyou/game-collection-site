Motorbike Stunt Simulator

By OnlineGames.io

Advertisement

Motorbike Stunt Simulator is a free 3D motorcycle game where you can explore dynamic stunt maps and perform the craziest and impossible tricks with your motorbike. 

Choose from four exciting stunt arenas, each filled with ramps, loops, obstacles or challenging courses. Master your bike's controls and rack up your stunt score by nailing the craziest flips, spins, and jumps.

Controls

WASD / Arrow Keys: Drive the bike
Spacebar: Handbrake
Shift: Activate boost
C: Switch camera views
R: Reset scene
G: Reset your bike
E: Turn on police lights
X: Hide instructions
Tab: Return to main menu

Tip: Want to switch bikes? Press the number keys (1–8) on your keyboard to choose from 8 different motorbike models, including a police motorcycle! Pick the one that suits your style best! 

Are you ready to ride your bike and become the king of the stunt world?
