Masked Special Forces

By OnlineGames.io

Advertisement

Masked Special Forces is a multiplayer first-person shooter game with a myriad of customization options. The game puts you in the shoes of a commander in the battle arena. As a talented warrior, team up, strategize, and take down the opponents one by one. 

Access to an array of upgradable weapons, armory, kill messages, and victory dances on the main menu. Ultra-realistic visuals and smooth gameplay will give you a feel at least as captivating as an iconic console game. 

In this fast-paced action game, you get to dive into an intense battle immediately. Or you can customize your gaming experience thoroughly before the gunfight. Select between available rooms, or create your own by selecting between detailed maps. 

Masked Special Forces is a game that stands out among all shooter games online thanks to its lifelike details. The game has been loved and played by thousands since its initial release. So, it happened to be a pioneer of a whole sequel with six different FPS games. How To Play Masked Special Forces

  • You can begin the game by joining a room made by other players, or you can create one yourself. 
  • To do that, you must give a name to the room, choose the maximum players, game mode, round time, bot count and map.
  • After entering a room, the battle starts immediately. Take your position and start eliminating enemies.
  • Learn about how to aim and shoot, other controls, display, armory, and maps below.

Controls

  • WASD or arrow keys to move
  • Left mouse click to fire
  • Right mouse click to aim
  • Space to jump
  • Q to slide while running
  • Left shift to run
  • 1, 2, 3 (or scroll) to change weapon
  • E or F to interact/pick up a weapon
  • G to throw available dynamite
  • H to use available medkits
  • T to chat
  • L to toggle cursor lock
  • C to crouch
  • Hold Tab to show the scoreboard
  • Escape to pause

HUD

  • BP & Health (displayed on the top left)
  • Kill feed (displayed on the top left)
  • Map (displayed on the top right)
  • Ammo (displayed on the bottom right)

Maps

The game has 6 insane modes you can choose from, and these are:

Profile Section

In the profile section, you will be able to change the nickname and load/save code (only experience can be saved through this code. It will include the following details: K/D Rate, Games Played, Total Play Time, Kills, Scores, Wins, Headshot Counts, Knife Kills, and Assist Counts.
