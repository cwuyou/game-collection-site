Mini Cars Racing

By OnlineGames.io

Mini Cars Racing is a 2 player car racing game you can play free on OnlineGames.io! You can race against the clock or battle a friend on the same device. It's fast, fun, and feels just like playing with toy cars on your childhood road traffic rug.

So, as you load the game, you have two options to choose from: Solo, where you race against CPU cars, or 2 Player (versus), a split-screen local multiplayer mode perfect for racing with a friend on the same keyboard. The controls are tight, the visuals are playful, and the tracks are wild and twisty.

There are two race types to master:

  • Arcade Mode: A classic 3-lap race against opponents. Outdrive them and cross the finish line first.
  • Time Trial Mode: Race solo against the clock and beat your own record. No rivals here.

You can also choose from three difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, and Expert. Expert mode will make the steering even more difficult in your mini car.

Controls

Player 1

  • Accelerate: W
  • Brake: S
  • Left / Right: A / D
  • Respawn: C

Player 2

  • Accelerate: Up Arrow
  • Brake: Down Arrow
  • Left / Right: Left Arrow / Right Arrow
  • Respawn: Keypad 1

How to Play Mini Cars Racing

  • Pick Solo or Versus mode from the main menu.
  • Choose your car among 8 funky mini cars, each with a different color and shape. From mini trucks to sporty vans, find your signature mini car.
  • Select your race mode: Arcade or Time Trial.
  • Choose one of three cool tracks:
    • Bridge Madness: Full of wild jumps and narrow bridges.
    • Cactus County: Desert-themed track with sharp turns.
    • Rock Island: A rocky, cliffside challenge with scenic views and deadly drops.
  • Adjust your difficulty, then hit Start and race your way to the top!
  • Expect obstacles like traffic cones and floating ramps. And don't worry, if you fall off the map, you'll respawn. And if you get stuck in a corner, just hit the respawn key and dive back into the race.

Developer

Mini Cars Racing was developed by FreezeNova, known for its fun and 2 player browser games. You can play this racing game online anytime on OnlineGames.io.
