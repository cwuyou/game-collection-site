What are Clicker Games?

Clicker Games are a genre of video games also known as incremental games or idle games. In clicker games, players point and click (or tap on mobile devices) to gather resources or collect points. In idle clickers or incremental games, clicking can be automated through in-game helpers.

However, the essence of clicker games lies in the rewarding process. These games have numerous rewards and unlockable features to indicate a player's progress.

Choose Your Action: Click or Tap?

You don't have to stick to clicking on a computer. Play clicker games on mobile devices! Most clicker games are compatible with iOS,Android and Chrome OS, allowing you to tap instead of clicking. Some clicker games have even taken their name from this feature.

Cookie Tap is an excellent example of this. In this game, you get to run a cookie factory and do your best to feed all the cookie monsters in the town.

The Addictive Nature of Clicker Games

There are basicly two aspects that make clicker games addictive:

  • constant progress
  • simple gameplay

What do we mean by constant progress? In clicker games, each click results in an increase of resources and make you closer to upgrade your units and automate the clicking. This constant cycle of progress and reward may keep you engaged and wanting more.

For example, in Idle Farming Business, you will start with a single crop of potato. And You will make your way to a farming empire with many different crops by clicking.

In an era of video games are getting more complicated and skill-based, you may need to blow off some steam playing a simple game with easy to achieve goals. This is where clicker games come into play.

In 2013 a game called Cookie Clicker proved that simple graphics and gameplay can be as at least fun as more complex games. If you'd like to play a cookie clicker game with a lovely Christmas theme, try Xmas Cookie Clicker. Join the clicker craze and see what all the fuss is about?
