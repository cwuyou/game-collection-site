Fast Food Rush

By OnlineGames.io

You're at Burger King, sinking your teeth into a juicy cheeseburger and finishing it off in about 15 minutes flat. But just this once, before you head out, pause and take a quick look around. Employees are rushing nonstop, flipping sizzling patties, taking orders at the counter, pouring ice-cold drinks, and wiping down tables. At this point, running your own burger joint might seem pretty appealing, at least to some people. Think about it: flipping burgers, serving happy customers, making money from your own fast food business. It all sounds simple, satisfying, and fun. But before you get overly excited and pour thousands of dollars into your burger empire dreams, how about giving it a test drive first? Let’s play a game. It’s called Fast Food Rush.

Here, you’ll experience firsthand what running your very own fast-food restaurant feels like. Starting small with Burger Blitz and 2,500 coins, you'll hire employees, unlock exciting upgrades, and turn your tiny burger stand into a full-on fast-food empire with 3 branches. Step into the world of burgers, coffee, and donuts, and discover just how far your American dream can take you!

Controls

On Computers

  • WASD / Arrow Keys / Drag Mouse: Move your character around
  • Mouse Scroll: Zoom in or out

On Mobile

  • Tap and Drag: Move your character

Start with the Burger Blitz

When you begin this free game, you’ll have 2,500 coins to get started. Your first goal is to unlock the first seating by stepping on the white marker showing the 75 $ cost. Then add a counter table so your customers can place orders and check out. Next walk over the 85$ sign to unlock the grill.  This will open the doors to serving your first customers. As soon as you start grilling burgers, bringing them to the counter and check out customers, coins start flowing in automatically.

Unlock Upgrades

Coins come in steadily when customers are happy, so make sure you spend them on the right upgrades. You’ll see white rectangles scattered around the restaurant. Walking on these markers let you invest in some upgrades:

  • Improve Grill Speed: Faster burger production means more burgers to sell.
  • Add/ Upgrade Checkout Table: Speed up how quickly customers pay.
  • Hire Cashiers: Automate the payment process so you can focus on bigger tasks.
  • Add/Upgrade Seating and Tables: More places to sit equals more customers served, which means bigger profits! Plus, you can gather more tips by upgrading to more comfortable seating.

Manage Employees

Once your burger operation is underway, walk over the room at the corner. You’ll unlock the HR manager there. She'll help you in hiring workers and boosting their skills. Walk in this room to increase your employees’ speed, carrying capacity, and overall efficiency. A well-trained team is the secret to raking in coins without breaking a sweat.

Lastly, unlock the general manager in the next door. He'll guide you improve your walking speed, capacity and profit rates.

Keep Tables Clean

A clean restaurant keeps customers happy. Make sure to walk around the dining area and clear dirty tables. If tables stay messy for too long, new customers won’t sit down, costing you sales. A quick swipe to clean up can make a big difference in your daily income.

Expand to New Branches

After your burger spot becomes a local hit, it’s time to grow! Go to map by clicking on the top left of your screen. You can unlock:

  • Coffee Shop: Serve yummy hot drinks. New upgrades, new machines, and a chance to grow your empire even more.
  • Donut Place: Finally, add sugary sweets to your menu. This branch is the last big step in your fast-food journey, offering a fresh set of challenges and money-making opportunities

Developer

Fast Food Rush was created by FreezeNova, a developer known for making enjoyable, casual titles. This free game stands out with its blend of idle-style income generation and active management, making it a great fit for anyone who loves a mix of strategy and quick fun.
