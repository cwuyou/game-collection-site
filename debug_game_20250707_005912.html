Monster Survivors

By OnlineGames.io

Monster Survivors is an free online survival game with a minimalistic design and roguelite elements. The monsters are here, and you must survive!  Aggressive crabs, eerie pumpkins, flesh-eating plants and yes, even evil bees!  But on a vast open land, there's no place to run or hide. All you can do is survive as long as you can until waves of monsters end your struggles. Gather loot in each run to buy upgrades, strengthen your survivor and help the next survivor stand longer.

Controls

Movement: Simply drag your mouse to move around the battlefield.

How to Play Monster Survivors

  • At the beginning of Monster Survivors, choose your weapon based on your preferred playstyle:
    • Steel Sword: Ideal for those who like to be in the thick of the action, slashing through enemies up close.
    • Wooden Wand: Perfect for players who prefer to attack from a distance with magical blasts.
  • Survival involves more than just fighting; you must also collect loot dropped by defeated enemies to gain experience and power up.
  • Standing still isn't an option, as you need to continuously move to scoop up valuable XP and avoid getting overwhelmed.
  • You can choose to run in big circles to keep enemies at bay while allowing you to collect XP efficiently.
  • When boss enemies appear, the play area will shrink. Be ready to dodge and fight in tighter spaces.
  • Monster Survivors features two distinct maps, each populated with unique monsters:
    • Dreamy Woods: Start your survival quest among hauntingly beautiful yet dangerous paths.
    • Shade Palace: Unlock this map by advancing far enough in Dreamy Woods, where even greater threats await.

When you get defeated, don’t worry, not everything goes to waste. Grab your gold coins and upgrade the survivors' armor, healing, damage and health levels in the main menu. These upgrades will stay persistent between sessions. The upgraded skills pass the next survivor to start a game strong. You can also unlock new characters, such as mages and warriors with higher health and damage ratios in the main menu.

Game Features

  • Rogulite Gameplay: Engage in non-stop action where every move and decision impacts your survival.
  • Upgrade System: During the game, as you collect XP, unlock enhancements that improve your attack power, speed, and survivor durability.
  • Boss Fights: Encounter intense boss battles that force you into close combat, testing your dodging skills and strategy in restricted spaces.
