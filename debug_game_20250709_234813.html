Stickman Parkour

By OnlineGames.io

Stickman Parkour is an online platformer game that you can play on your browser, embracing the easy-to-learn but hard-to-master mechanics. Collect keys, open chests, pass levels and help him find his way out across 30 levels.

What is Stickman Parkour about?

Imagine Stickman, a tiny stick figure who decides one day to go on this epic adventure. He's on a mission to find these super special golden keys. These keys aren't just for opening doors; they unlock these ancient, mysterious gates that might be hiding treasures as cool as a room full of candy! Stickman doesn't have to worry about school or chores, so he's all in for this adventure.

His journey is full of crazy challenges like dodging sharp spikes, swinging on ropes like a monkey, and even swimming like he's in a stick figure Olympics. Each key he finds opens a new gate, leading to more challenges. The big dream? To find the very last gate with the final golden key in his pocket!

Controls

For Computers:

  • Move: Left/Right Arrows or A/D
  • Jump: Up Arrow/W
  • Slide/Dive: Down Arrow/S

For Mobiles:

  • Use on-screen buttons to control Stickman

How to Play Stickman Parkour

  • Use your keyboard or the buttons on the screen to make Stickman jump over dangers, swing on ropes, and swim through water. It's like playing a superhero in a stick-figure world!
  • Your job is to help Stickman grab these golden keys. They're like the ultimate treasure map pieces!
  • Get Stickman to the end of each level, which is marked by a funny squiggle.

Developer

Stickman Parkour game was created by FreezeNova Games Studio.

Explore More Adventures

If you're all about Stickman's high-flying escapades, you'll definitely want to try Fancy Pants. In this side-scrolling game, you control a stick figure with an epic hairdo and even fancier pants, running through worlds that are as wild as Stickman's obstacle course but with its own unique twist of humor and style. It's like if Stickman decided to get a wardrobe upgrade and a new set of challenges.
